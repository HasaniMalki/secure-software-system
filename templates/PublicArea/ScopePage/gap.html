Research Gap

Review of literature suggests that a number of studies have been used decision tree algorithm to mining data and classify data. Moreover, most of these studies have been mainly undertaken to predict the outcome based on the most common data factors.



One of the above literature suggests a system using algorithms to find the perfect donor. But there is some differentiation between this system and the HEART system. This system has been focused to find one suitable donor to blood retrieve and inform the donor using a call system. But in case of emergency blood bank has to find more than one donor to get blood. In HEART blood bank management system, around 20 best donors will be selected by using the algorithms.


Most of the hospitals and the blood bank centres have not solution to find the best donors in an emergency situation. If they need to find the best donors they need to activate manually. Some blood bank centres have an immediate broadcasting system to use at this kind of situation. When an emergency situation blood bank centre can publish the situation and inform people. So then people can donate blood. This procedure gets too much time and has to spend the more unnecessary cost on that. But In HEART system, most suitable donors will be selected and send a message for each selected member about the donation and get their response quickly. That will increase the response rate of a particular blood donor. So the blood donation centre has to care about only collect the blood from the donors. This is a faster procedure than the above mentioned procedure and the cost also very low.

And there is one more important thing to consider. The blood bank centre has to consider whether the blood bank centre located in a busier place or not. If the blood bank centre is located in a busier place and needs to get blood immediately, it is not suitable to find best donors too far from the centre. Because it is too hard to reach to the blood donation centre due to heavy traffic. As an example, if a blood donation centre in Colombo needs immediate blood collection, it is not suitable to inform donors in more than 10 km from the centre. But if the blood donation centre is located in a normal place, it is not a big issue to find and inform donors within 10 â€“ 15 km from the centre. Currently, there is no system to solve this problem. But HEART system can give a solution for that. In this system, there is an option to select a stage and each stage cover several distances from the blood donation centre. The blood donation centre can select stage deservedly.
