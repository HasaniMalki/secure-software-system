Research Problem

Each year, about 60,000 Americans die from hemorrhaging, or blood loss. Worldwide, that number is almost 2 million. Nearly 1.25 million people die in road crashes each year, on average 3,287 deaths a day. An additional 20-50 million are injured or disabled. And also, there are a huge number of deaths happened because of natural disasters. Like these kinds of situations, patients need a blood emergency. The blood bank should be capable of providing the required blood from the hospital at the very moment. Globally, 4.2 million people are estimated to die within 30 days of surgery each year. In emergency surgery, the hospital has to find suitable blood collection as soon as possible. A patient’s death might be affected by a lack of blood supply. In case of emergency, the blood donation centre has to find suitable and best blood donors to blood retrieve. And also if the suitable blood donors are selected but the blood bank centre could not inform the donors and get their response quickly, it can be big issue in an emergency situation. In case of this type of situation, the blood donation centre needs a fast method to inform donors and get their response immediately. Considering all the above facts, It is clear that really need a solution for finding the best donors in an emergency situation.


According to the research gap, it was discovered that these research questions will cover the entire scope of the proposed system.


• Collecting blood in an emergency situation is a very important as patients' life is dependent on it.

• Most of the hospitals do not have efficient methods to implement in such situations.

• Lack of communication and absence of proper technology solutions has led to these difficulties in finding required amounts of blood at needed times.

• Even if the blood bank manages to identify the suitable blood donors, there is no proper mechanism to inform the donors and get their response quickly.

• It can be a big issue in emergency situations.

• Therefore, the blood donation centre needs a fast method to inform donors and get their response immediately.
